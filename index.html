✦ The World

A Planet of
Strange Beauty

Florbia is a planet unlike any other — alive with colour, cosmic energy, and creatures that defy explanation. Four ancient races call it home, each inhabiting their own corner of this wild, weird world. They coexist in an unspoken peace... for now. The borders are blurring. The encounters are beginning.

✦ The Four Races

Who Are You?

Kibiri

Round, squishy blob bodies with stubby little legs. The Kibiri are gentle cosmic wanderers, surprisingly resilient, and deceptively hard to knock over.

Blob · Round · Cosmic
Orbyn

Reptilian and rough around the edges. The Orbyn are ancient lizard-folk who've seen every war Florbia has ever forgotten. Don't mistake calm for weakness.

Reptile · Ancient · Fierce
Nebra

Ghostly and fluffy, the Nebra drift between the visible and the unseen. Their fur absorbs starlight. Their eyes hold galaxies. Their intentions? Known only to them.

Ghost · Fluffy · Mystic
Lyric

Tall, smooth, and oddly elegant. The Lyric speak in patterns no other race fully understands. They wear the cosmos on their bodies and carry the universe in their silence.

Smooth · Tall · Enigmatic
