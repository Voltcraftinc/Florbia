✦ The World

A planet of
strange beauty

Florbia is alive. It breathes, glows, and hums with energy that no other planet has. Four ancient races have carved out their own corners of it, living in their own way, on their own terms. Nobody told them to get along. Nobody had to. The planet holds a peace that just sort of works. For now.

✦ The Four Races

Who are you?

Kibiri

Big, pale, soft-bodied creatures with long tendrils hanging down like they're not quite attached to the ground. Smooth and almost ghostly on top, a wild burst of colour and pattern below. Nobody really knows what's going on inside a Kibiri. The Kibiri are fine with that.

Pale · Strange · Unreadable
Lyric

Dense, fuzzy, and covered in wiggly texture from head to foot. The Lyric look like something that grew rather than was born. Stubby legs, round body, yellow dots scattered all over like a map of somewhere. Unbothered by pretty much everything.

Fuzzy · Grounded · Stubborn
Nebra

A perfect round ball with a gloss to them like they swallowed a piece of sky. Smooth, blue, almost completely spherical with tiny legs poking out the bottom that somehow do the job. The Nebra don't overcomplicate things. They just exist, and that's enough.

Round · Smooth · Effortless
Orbyn

Stocky grey bodies with a thick cascade of wild white fur that swamps everything from the waist up. Wide sturdy feet. Low centre of gravity. The Orbyn look like they've been through some things and came out completely fine. Hard to move. Hard to shake. Hard to ignore.

Stocky · Wild · Unmovable

In the beginning, there was Florbia

Nobody built it. Nobody seeded it. Florbia just sort of appeared at the edge of a galaxy that hadn't been named yet, glowing faintly and humming at a frequency that felt, to anything alive, like home.

The four races didn't arrive together. They didn't even arrive from the same direction. But they all arrived. And when they found each other, something clicked. Not friendship exactly. Something older than that.

The planet chose them. That much everyone agrees on. Why it chose these four, in these shapes, with these abilities, nobody has ever been able to explain. Some things just are.

The Unspoken Agreement

Nobody wrote it down. Nobody shook hands over it. The peace between the four races was never negotiated because it never needed to be. Each race found their place and stayed in it.

The Kibiri settled the soft coastal edges where the planet glows brightest. The Orbyn took the ancient stone ridges in the deep interior. The Nebra drifted through the upper cloud layers where no one else could follow. The Lyric claimed the crystalline valleys that hum with low frequency sound.

Four worlds within one world. It worked because nobody pushed. The planet seemed to reward the balance.

The Edges Are Getting Blurry

Something shifted. Nobody noticed at first because it was gradual, the way all important things are. The borders between regions started to soften. The crystal valleys drifted closer to the stone ridges. The cloud layers sank lower than they had in a thousand years.

For the first time, a Kibiri woke up one morning to find an Orbyn standing in their garden. Not threatening. Not hostile. Just there. Looking around. Taking it all in.

These encounters are becoming more common. They're not violent. But they're charged with something. A tension that everyone is pretending not to feel.

The Cute Wars

Nobody was going to start a real war. That was never going to happen on Florbia. The planet doesn't allow it. But the tension needed somewhere to go, and what emerged was something nobody could have predicted.

Challenges. Tiny battles between individuals. A Nebra drifting into a Kibiri settlement and issuing what could only be described as a very polite but very serious challenge. A crowd forming. Someone keeping score.

The Cute Wars aren't wars at all. They're sport. They're performance. They're the planet's way of letting everyone compete without anyone getting hurt. And somehow, impossibly, they've become the most important thing on Florbia.

What Happens Next

The battles are getting more organised. Classes are forming. Allegiances are being made across racial lines for the first time in Florbian history. A Kibiri Moonblade fighting alongside an Orbyn Starshard against a pair of Nebra Mistlings.

Nobody knows how far this goes. The archives are filling with records of contests and outcomes and new rivalries. Something big is building.

The Glowpuffs are starting to change hands. The first real economy is forming. And somewhere in the cloud layers, the Nebra are watching all of it very carefully and saying absolutely nothing.
